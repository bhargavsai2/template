About Us

Generations are changing. Technology is also changing day by day. Then why can’t saving of money change according to this technology? This thought inspired us to create something new. And here is OINKY the piggy bank

According to this generation, spending money is very easy but, saving money is not easy. Nevertheless, we provide the option to save for some cause/occasion with the help of Oinky bank.

Statistics reveal that Indians spend most of their income on food, transport, housing, education and other necessities. Apart from this, the whole lot of money is being wasted unproductively which in turn leads them to take loans.

Technology has taken over our society today and we intend to introduce a healthy lifestyle to our customers through Oinky.

The time you spend your money you may forget your limits but you got to plan first and be rational before you just give your money to something that is just as good as a scrap. This is for a generation exists right now.

Transfer

Oinky has two wallets. One is the open wallet and the other is safe lock wallet.

You can make unlimited transfers from the open wallet. But only limited transfers from safe lock wallet.

You can also fix a target date on which you have to make a transaction while saving money in your safe lock wallet and give the account details to which the amount has to be transfered to make automatic transactions from your safe lock wallet.
